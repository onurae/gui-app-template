1. after building, the binary_to_compressed_c.exe will be created here.
2. open the command prompt (cmd)
3. example: binary_to_compressed_c.exe myfont.ttf MyFont > myfont.cpp

binary_to_compressed_c.exe Roboto-2.138/Roboto-Medium.ttf RobotoMedium > RobotoMedium.cpp
binary_to_compressed_c.exe Roboto-2.138/Roboto-Regular.ttf RobotoRegular > RobotoRegular.cpp
binary_to_compressed_c.exe MaterialIcons-7Jan23/MaterialIcons-Regular.ttf MaterialIcons > MaterialIcons.cpp
